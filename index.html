Hi, I'm Hayden.

I'm an incoming MASc student in the Vision and Image Processing (VIP) Lab at the University of Waterloo. My interests include deep learning, medical imaging, and computational imaging. I previously received my BASc in Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

Currently, I collaborate on the COVID-Net initiative, focusing on detection and analysis methods for COVID-19 in chest X-ray and CT images. Most recently, I worked as a Research Engineer in Huawei's Human-Machine Interaction Lab, focusing on generative adversarial networks for image synthesis. When I'm not busy with school or work, I like to paddle, hike, ski, and work on personal projects.
